I conceived the concept for the Sonria program as a project for a senior class elective and assumed the role of team manager to oversee a team of three female collaborators, with my primary responsibility being the design and implementation of the journal and forums pages. Additionally, I provided valuable input and coordinated the establishment of our database infrastructure, utilizing Firebase.
My work
Sonria! is designed to provide comprehensive support for college students in multiple aspects of their lives. The app includes features such as a mood tracker, a daily planner, a task manager, as study manager, a community forum, and resources for mental health topics, such as articles and advice. The goal of the web-app is to provide students with the tools they need to succeed academically, emotionally, and personally, and to reduce the stress and challenges that often accompany college life.
